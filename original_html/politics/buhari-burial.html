Former President Buhari’s Remains Laid to Rest

Published on July 15, 2025 • Politics

The remains of former President Muhammadu Buhari have been laid to rest at his residence in Daura, Katsina State. The funeral took place amid a heavy military presence and a gathering of top government officials.

The late president’s body, draped in the Nigerian flag, arrived at his residence around 4:33 p.m., accompanied by his successor, President Bola Ahmed Tinubu, as well as top political and military elites.

At approximately 5:50 p.m., the former president was lowered into the grave by Vice President Kashim Shettima and other dignitaries present at the solemn ceremony.

Nigerians around the world have continued to pay tribute to the former leader, who served from 2015 to 2023 and was known for his anti-corruption stance and strong governance.

"His legacy will live on in the hearts of many Nigerians," said one of the mourners.
