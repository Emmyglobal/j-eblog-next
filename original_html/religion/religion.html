The Beauty of Thanksgiving: Lessons from Psalms

Religion • July 12, 2025
Psalms 92:1
"How good it is to give thanks to you, O Lord, to sing in your honour, O Most High God."

If you study the book of Psalms very closely, you will discover that more than seventy percent of the contents are on prayers for help, protection and defence.

This evidences the fact that King David, one of the prominent authors of this Bible book, did not find life all rosy; yet, he saw the beauty in thanksgiving to God.

Let us learn from David today.

If you still have the breath of life, quit complaining and give thanks.
