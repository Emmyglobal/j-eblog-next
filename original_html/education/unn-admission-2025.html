UNN Releases 2025 Admission List

Brought to you by J-E Blog — Stay Updated, Stay Informed

The University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN) has released the 2025 primary admission list. Candidates who applied for admission can now check their status on the university’s official portal.

Important Details:

  • Admission List: The 2025 primary list is now available online.
  • Check Status: Visit the official UNN website to confirm your admission.
  • Acceptance Fee: A non-refundable fee of ₦30,000 is required.
  • Payment Instructions: Use the correct invoice number and retain your confirmation slip.

Admission Requirements

UNN typically requires a minimum combined score of 200+ from UTME and Post-UTME. However, candidates with scores below 200 may still be considered if they chose UNN as their first choice and selected eligible courses.

Age Requirement

Candidates must be at least 16 years old to be eligible for admission into any Nigerian tertiary institution, including UNN.
