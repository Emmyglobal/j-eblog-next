Covenant University Post UTME 2025 – Guidelines & Application

Covenant University, one of Nigeria’s leading private universities, has opened its Post UTME application portal for the 2025/2026 academic session. If you're considering admission into this prestigious institution, here’s everything you need to know.

Eligibility

  • Candidate must have chosen Covenant University as their first choice.
  • Minimum JAMB score: 180
  • Possess at least five credit passes in relevant subjects (including English and Mathematics)

How to Apply

  1. Visit the Covenant University admission portal: admission.covenantuniversity.edu.ng
  2. Click on “Apply for Admission” and create an account.
  3. Complete the application form and upload required documents (JAMB result, O’Level, passport, etc.).
  4. Pay the ₦7,000 application fee online.
  5. Schedule your screening test date.

Screening Format

The CU Post UTME test is computer-based and covers:

  • Logical Reasoning
  • English Language
  • Mathematics (for science applicants)
  • General Knowledge

Important Dates

The application portal opens in July 2025 and closes in August 2025. Screening dates will be communicated via email and the portal.
