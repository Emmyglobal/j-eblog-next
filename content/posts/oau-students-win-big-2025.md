# **OAU Students Achieve Double Global & National Victory in 2025**

Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU) has once again stamped its name boldly on both the national and global academic map. In an inspiring display of brilliance, **OAU students dominated the 2025 NCDMB National Essay Competition** and also recorded a groundbreaking achievement as **three Nigerian students became global winners of the IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS) Scholarship**.

Both achievements highlight the intellectual strength, discipline, and innovation deeply rooted in OAU’s academic culture.

---

#  **1. OAU Student Wins the 2025 NCDMB National Essay Competition**

This year’s **Nigerian Content Development & Monitoring Board (NCDMB)** National Undergraduate Essay Competition saw exceptional entries from universities nationwide. Rising above thousands of submissions, an OAU student emerged as **one of the top national winners**, solidifying the university’s reputation for producing exceptional thinkers and writers.

The student received:

- A **scholarship prize**
- National recognition from NCDMB
- Exclusive access to mentorship and innovation opportunities in Nigeria’s energy sector

This win reflects OAU’s commitment to nurturing students who combine sharp thinking, creativity, and national consciousness.

---

#  **2. Three OAU Students Named Global IEEE SPS Scholars (Only Africans Selected)**

In an even more historic moment, **three Nigerian students** — all from OAU — were selected as winners of the prestigious **IEEE Signal Processing Society Scholarship**.  
This award is given to exceptional globally competitive students driving innovation in:

- Artificial intelligence  
- Data and signal processing  
- Communication systems  
- Computing and engineering research  

### **Meet the Awardees**

### **1. Lawal Damilare Emmanuel — SPS Scholar**
A brilliant engineering mind listed among the global SPS Scholars.

### **2. Emmanuel Ameh Peter — SPS Scholar**
Recognized for his excellence in computation, algorithms, and research depth.

### **3. Mujeeb Olajide Opabode — SPS Scholar**
Celebrated globally for research potential, leadership, and academic excellence.

They were the **only Africans selected globally**, showcasing OAU’s exceptional talent on the world stage.
